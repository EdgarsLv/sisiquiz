Sociotype explanations

In Socionics, each personality type has several names:

  • Title – a descriptive role (e.g., Provider)
  • Type code – the four-letter abbreviation (e.g., ESFJ)
  • Nickname – a traditional Socionics name, often from famous writers or figures (e.g., Hugo)
